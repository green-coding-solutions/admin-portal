How can my organisation be included in the dataset?

Hosting providers can submit a free verification request to us to demonstrate they are green. All verified hosting providers are included in the Green Domains dataset for free.

Once verified, hosting providers can further support our mission to green the internet by 2030, by paying a modest fee to be included in our Green Hosting Directory.

Whichever route you choose, the starting point is the same - use our verification request form.
