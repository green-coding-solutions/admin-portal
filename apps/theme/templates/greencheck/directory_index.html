About this directory ℹ

The Green Web Directory is a searchable list which enables people to quickly find verified green hosting companies. These are organisations that offer hosting services and can demonstrate they are taking steps to avoid, reduce or offset the greenhouse gas emissions caused by using electricity to provide their services. To become verified they share evidence with us, Green Web Foundation, to show they're doing this on a yearly basis, or better. Verification is free.
